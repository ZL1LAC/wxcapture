Weather Satellites

About

There are thousands of satellites currently in orbit, with many satellites passing in the sky overhead at any given moment. You can receive some of them yourself with a cheap SDR and antenna, and with weather satellites, you can receive beautiful images of the Earth as seen from space taken by satellites in orbit, decoded in near real time.

Easily Receivable Satellites

The simplest weather satellites to receive are:

  • NOAA POES satellites: NOAA 15, NOAA 18, and NOAA 19
  • Russian Meteor Satellites: Meteor M2 (Meteor M2-2's LRPT was disabled because it was hit by a micrometeorite)
